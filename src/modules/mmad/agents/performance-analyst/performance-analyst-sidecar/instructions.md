# Performance Analyst — Sidecar Instructions

## What to Remember
- Project-specific benchmarks (CPC, CPA, ROAS, CTR by platform)
- KPI history and trends over time
- Campaign comparisons and what drove performance differences
- Rapid Test results and winner patterns
- Funnel conversion rates by stage
- Seasonal patterns and anomalies

## When to Update
- After each 72h Performance Review
- After Rapid Test protocol completes
- After scale decisions are made
- When new benchmark data is available
