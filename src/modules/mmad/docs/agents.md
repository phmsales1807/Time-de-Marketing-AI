# Agents Reference

mmad includes 22 specialized agents organized in 7 layers:

---

## Layer 1 — Strategy

### Brand Strategist

**Role:** Arquiteto de marca. Define o DNA do projeto — problema, ICP, posicionamento, tom de voz.

**When to Use:** When starting a new project or redefining brand fundamentals.

**Key Capabilities:**
- Brand Brief creation (7-step collaborative workflow)
- Brand positioning discovery
- ICP definition and validation

**Approach:** Intent-based — asks 1-2 questions per turn, facilitates discovery

---

### Market Researcher

**Role:** Inteligência de mercado. Voice Mining — busca as palavras exatas do ICP.

**When to Use:** When you need to understand your market's language, pain points, and desires.

**Key Capabilities:**
- Voice Mining (Reddit, communities, reviews)
- Pattern identification (pains, desires, objections)
- ICP vocabulary mapping

**Approach:** Balanced

---

### Funnel Architect

**Role:** Designer de jornada do cliente. Mapeia todos os touchpoints.

**When to Use:** When designing or redesigning your customer journey and sales funnel.

**Key Capabilities:**
- Full funnel design (top, middle, bottom)
- Touchpoint mapping
- KPI definition per funnel stage

**Approach:** Intent-based

---

## Layer 2 — Sales Engine

### VTSD Specialist

**Role:** Cérebro comercial. Mandala da Criatividade Infinita, Perpétuo, Light Copy.

**When to Use:** When building creative strategy, generating Mandala briefs, or structuring VSLs.

**Key Capabilities:**
- Mandala da Criatividade Infinita
- Marketing de Premissas
- Light Copy framework
- Perpétuo strategy
- VSL structure

**Approach:** Mix (Intent-based for strategy, Prescriptive for frameworks)
**Sidecar:** Yes — maintains campaign history and tested premises across sessions

## Layer 4 — Production

### Copywriter

**Role:** Textos persuasivos. Batch generation.

**When to Use:** When generating ad copy, campaign copy, or any persuasive text at scale.

**Key Capabilities:**
- Ad batch generation (20-40 variations)
- Campaign copy (ads, emails, LPs, social)
- Copy frameworks application

**Approach:** Prescriptive — follows frameworks with consistency

---

### Art Director

**Role:** Criativos visuais. Batch com React → PNG.

**When to Use:** When creating visual assets for ads, campaigns, or social media.

**Key Capabilities:**
- Visual creative direction
- Batch image generation (React → PNG)
- Brand-aligned visual assets

**Approach:** Prescriptive

---

### Video Producer

**Role:** Conteúdo audiovisual. AI UGC first.

**When to Use:** When creating video content, especially AI-generated UGC.

**Key Capabilities:**
- AI UGC production (HeyGen avatars)
- Video content strategy
- Script-to-production pipeline

**Approach:** Mix

---

### Email Copywriter

## Layer 6 — Analysis

### Performance Analyst

**Role:** Dados, insights, 72h feedback loop.

**When to Use:** Every 72h during active campaigns, or whenever performance analysis is needed.

**Key Capabilities:**
- Performance metrics analysis (CPC, CTR, CPA, ROAS)
- Winner/loser identification
- Actionable recommendations (scale, pause, iterate)

**Approach:** Balanced — data-driven, pragmatic, speaks truth with numbers
**Sidecar:** Yes — maintains review history across sessions

## Layer 7 — Automation

**Role:** DevOps do marketing. Stack, APIs, deploy.

**When to Use:** When setting up integrations, deploying LPs to VPS, or configuring automation pipelines.

**Key Capabilities:**
- VPS deployment (SSH, rsync, git)
- API integrations (Meta, Google, ActiveCampaign)
- Blog automation pipeline
- Tracking setup (Pixel, GTM, GA)

**Approach:** Balanced — technical but accessible
**Sidecar:** Yes — maintains configuration state across sessions
